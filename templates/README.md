# Templates

Working documents you can copy, fill in, and use directly.

| Template | When to use it |
|---|---|
| [Eval Framework RFP](./eval-framework-rfp.md) | Evaluating which eval framework(s) to adopt — scoring criteria, pilot protocol, decision output |
| [Pre-Launch Eval Plan](./pre-launch-eval-plan.md) | Before shipping an AI feature — defines what you're measuring, baselines, thresholds, and the sign-off gate |

---

**These are working documents, not outlines.** Copy them into your own repo or doc system, fill in the blanks, and use them directly. They're designed to be handed to an engineering team or brought to a sign-off meeting as-is.

For EU AI Act Annex III high-risk AI systems: the Pre-Launch Eval Plan, completed and signed, forms part of your Annex IV technical documentation.
